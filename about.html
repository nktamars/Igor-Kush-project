About

Igor Volodymyrovych. (date of birth: 20 September 1966) - Ukrainian financial industry expert and philanthropist.

His current research interests include: investment in banking sector, development, agricultural business, recruitment, ecology, medicine, ICO and blockchain projects.

Education

First university degree he got at Voroshilovgrad Machine and Equipment Institute. Second university degree received at Ivan Franko National University of Lviv [12]. In Kharkiv National University of Internal Affairs defended the dissertation on the topic: “Anti-corruption policy of the modern state: theoretical and comparative legal characteristic”. Obtained Ph.D. in Law. From 2018 received Ph.D. in Political Science. He defended his doctoral dissertation on the topic "Political corruption: comparative-political science conceptualization".

Professional Experience

Founder of the International Investment Holding SVH since 2010, with its total investment in amount of $ 158 million . Controller of two Ukrainian banks: “FINANCE BANK” and “INVESTMENT TRUST BANK” - with total regulatory capital in amount of 336.4 million UAH.

Regular expert at the Community Council of State Fiscal Service and of Ministry of Internal Affairs of Ukraine.

Founder of "Kushnarov Attorney Bureau", which is well-known for its international human rights activity. As a part of SVH Private Banking Agency each year organizes a Private Banking Summit.

Cooperates with large financial EU groups. Official agent of some of them.

According to "Platnik podatkіv" magazine Igor Kushnarov and his family are the largest taxpayers among individuals in Ukraine. For the period of 2010-2014 family of Kushnarov paid taxes over 30.3 million UAH.

Titles and Awards

2013 – winner of the "Lawyer of the year".

Public Activities

Provides free legal assistance for the SME (small and medium business) and disadvantaged citizens.

Provides assistance to the orphan asylum for disabled.

Active participant of the civil society movement for the environmental protection “Native Planet Public Organization”.

Journalist, author of over 300 magazine articles and author of the monography on taxation and corruption.
