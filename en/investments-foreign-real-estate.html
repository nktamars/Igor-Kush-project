Investments in foreign real estate (Europe, USA).

19.01.2018

Our company does not stand still to improve the quality of services for our present and target customers and based on market analysis is ready to offer the best investment instruments. We would like to share with you the news about new secure investment instrument – purchase of foreign real estate in Spain (Costa Blanca) and in USA (Florida). In the end of 2017 SVH representatives had the opportunity to participate in two days exhibition in Budapest (Hungary), where investment projects in these regions were presented directly by the developers’ representatives. The managing director of the company personally made the presentation of development company Euromarina (Alicante, Spain), during which the participants could get acquainted with the long history of the company, its spotless reputation, construction geography and, of course, receive exhaustive information about investment objects. As of today the company Euromarina offers apartments, duplexes, villas from 60 to 400 sq. m for realization. The particular attention was attracted by the project of Villa Amaris, cozy modern villas with two-three bedrooms that perfectly combine minimalism in Mediterranean style.

If Spain is an option for those, who would like to use the real estate more for the purpose of whole year recreation, the projects, presented by the company Dezer Development (Florida, USA) are more about investments. Complexes Casa Armani (interior design was personally developed by Giorgio Armani), Hyde Beach House (sales of objects with guaranteed hotel program and further leasing), Porsche Design Tower (apartments with fantastic ocean view and highly equipped technics) will meet the demands of any customer.

The organizers and the general partners in selection and support of possible transactions are the company Prestige Sapphire International located in Budapest, the main profile of which is sale of foreign real estate of new construction only and further investment consulting. The head and the founder of the company Prestige Sapphire International, Dr. Czibere Ilona, has more than 20 years of experience in the sphere of investments in foreign real estate, and also local lawyers and tax consultants in Spain and United States, who are ready to fully support transactions and answer to all questions of the customers.

In case of interest or additional questions, our specialist and partners are at your service.
